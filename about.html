ABOUT

CodeBloom Infotech was started with a big dream to help people become powerful through education and innovation. We're like a shining example of excellence right here in Nepalgunj. Our team is made up of teachers who know their stuff and software experts who are really good at what they do. We all love technology and believe it can change lives. Our promise is to give top-notch education and make our clients really happy. We're always growing and getting better, creating an environment where learning, creativity, and being the best is what we're all about. If you're a student wanting to get better at what you do, or a business looking for super cool solutions in the digital world, come join us on this exciting journey. We're here to make learning and technology awesome for everyone!
